This website is currently suspended pending a review.

If you would like to report an empty home in England, please contact your local council and ask for the contact details for the person who works on empty homes. You can also use the following link to report a derelict or abandoned building to the building control section of the local council: www.gov.uk/report-derelict-abandoned-building

If you would like to report an empty home in Scotland and need to find out who to contact please visit the Shelter Scotland empty homes pages for a list of local Empty Homes Officers. If your council area doesn't appear on the Shelter Scotland site or you are having trouble directing your report to the right council contact please email the Scottish Empty Homes Partnership at emptyhomes@shelter.org.uk

If you would like to report an empty homes in Wales, you can continue to report empty homes via the Shelter Cymru website: www.sheltercymru.org.uk/projects/empty-homes/
